TechCon: A Legacy of Innovation

TechCon was founded in 2012 with a mission to empower African tech talent and foster innovation across the continent. Since then, we've hosted groundbreaking conferences featuring leading figures like Dr. Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala and Iyinoluwa Aboyeji.

Our Mission

  • To provide a platform for African tech leaders, entrepreneurs, and developers to connect, share ideas, and collaborate.
  • To showcase the latest advancements in African technology and inspire the next generation of innovators.
  • To advocate for policies that promote a thriving tech ecosystem in Africa.

Past Speakers

  • Fred Swaniker: Founder of the African Leadership Group, Swaniker is a renowned entrepreneur and education advocate. TechCon hosted him in 2023 where he shared insights on leadership and innovation. Swaniker highlighted the empowering the youth with tech skills to become change agents in the society. He said that Technology is our passport to a better society and Africa must not be left behind.
  • Mark Zuckerberg: The Facebook founder needs no introduction. His keynote in 2023 focused on the role of social media in shaping the tech landscape.
